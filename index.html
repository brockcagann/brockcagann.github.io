Brock Elliot Cagann

Graduating from high school in 2002, Brock went to Arizona State University to study his lifelong love of architecture but the summer before junior year he was in a near-fatal car accident. Required to stay in Indianapolis for therapy and discouraged with what architecture school had become, Brock attended Herron school of art and rediscovered his second love, painting.
